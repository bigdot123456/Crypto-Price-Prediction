Analysis



Candlestick Chart: To better visualize the fluctuations in bitcoin price per day. Moving Averages: 10-day moving average. When the price crosses below the moving average, it signals a bearish price action, and vice versa.
Bollinger Bands: Many traders believe the closer the prices move to the upper band, the more overbought in the market, and the closer the prices move to the lower band, the more oversold in the market.

Predictions
